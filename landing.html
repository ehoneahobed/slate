Everything, one surface

Draw, type, embed, explain.

Slate bundles the best of Xournal++, Jamboard, Miro and OneNote — without the split-personality. One notebook that thinks like a teacher.

A real pen, not a simulation

Pressure-sensitive ink, palm rejection, and wobbly-circle-to-clean-circle shape recognition. Write the way you've always written.

Pages, sections, notebooks

A OneNote-style spine, but scrolls like a book. Switch any page to ruled, grid, or plain in a click.

Drop anything in

Images, videos, YouTube, audio, PDFs, LaTeX, code, tables, stickies. Then annotate on top.

Share like a book, not a file

One link. Three modes: read-only, read + personal annotations, or live collab. Students pick up right where you left off.

Optional AI, your key

Summaries, quiz generation, handwriting → text. Bring your own OpenAI/Anthropic key, or turn it off entirely.

Works everywhere

Web, iPad, Surface, desktop wrapper. Your notebook syncs; your ink stays crisp.
